About Me

I'm Rajat and I'm an Illustrator

I began my journey as an entrepreneur from the age of 17 where I created YouTube videos as a side hobby around consumer tech products, comedy, and music. It was also around this time I was in engineering college. Having some online presence also got me inbound freelance opportunities and I was making money as a student. Later I used this money to start a drop shipping store which I could nearly break-even at that point. But I could be called a marketer as I had not just learned marketing but I had to spend money and executed on it. The drop shipping store reached 100,000+ impression and then shut it down considering I had to focus on my final year. During my final year, I did a research project on “The problems Influencers face getting paid”, of course along with my engineering projects.
